ABOUT

Our project uses the Sloan Digital Sky Survey data to predict galaxy subclasses. Learn about the data, the machine learning model, and how it all works together.


The Sloan Digital Sky Survey provides an extensive catalog of astronomical data.
Our machine learning model processes this data to classify galaxies into 'STARFORMING' or 'STARBURST'
categories based on their photometric characteristics. Explore the data and insights to understand the universe better.
